PNSync is a Learning Management System Website made specifically for Grade 12 students of The University of Cabuyao (UC-PnC) for the Academic Year 2023-2024. As a prototype for the capstone product of Group 3 Researchers from 12ESTEM-C6, PNSync offers different modules of varrying subjects of ESTEM-C students. From Major subjects to Core subjects, all subjects are offered with different modules to further enhance students' learning options.
